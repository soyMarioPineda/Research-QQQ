# Anatomy of the Retest in the QQQ Opening Range Breakout

Exploratory study of what happens in the minutes *after* a QQQ Opening Range Breakout.  
All prior literature enters on the breakout. This paper measures what comes next.

---

## What this paper documents

- Most breakouts pull back to retest the broken level before continuing
- The faster that retest happens, the more likely price is to continue in the original direction
- A wider opening range tends to produce a larger move after the breakout
- A large move before the retest is associated with lower continuation probability
No claims of causality. No claims of profitability. Just measuring what happens, precisely enough for others to replicate or challenge it.

---

## Data

- Instrument: Invesco QQQ Trust ETF
- Resolution: 1-minute OHLCV bars
- Period: January 2017 – December 2024
- Session: 9:30 AM – 3:59 PM Eastern Time
